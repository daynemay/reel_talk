# Privacy Policy for reel talk 🎬

**Last updated:** July 2, 2026

**reel talk 🎬** does not collect, store, or share any personal data. 

**reel talk 🎬** runs entirely on your device and does not transmit any information to external servers.

## Permissions

**reel talk 🎬** uses text-to-speech (TTS) functionality provided by your device's built-in speech engine. No audio is recorded or transmitted.
